escape(str) click to toggle source

Applies operator escape’s according to OPERATORS_ESCAPE_TABLE. 

  op_esc('-')    #=> "op_minus"
  op_esc('odd?') #=> "odd-Q"

  def self.escape(str)
    str.gsub(OPERATORS_REGEXP){ OPERATORS_ESC_TABLE[$1] }.sub(/\?$/, '\1-Q')
  end
